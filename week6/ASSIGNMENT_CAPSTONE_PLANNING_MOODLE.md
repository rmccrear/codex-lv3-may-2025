# Capstone Project Planning Assignment

## Objective
Complete the capstone project planning worksheet before beginning development. This planning phase is essential for creating a successful project that meets all requirements.

---

## Assignment Overview

You will complete a comprehensive planning document that guides you through:
- Brainstorming your app ideas
- Selecting and exploring datasets from Code.org
- Wireframing your application screens
- Planning your database tables and relationships
- Setting daily goals and milestones
- Creating a technical checklist

**Or use the markdown version:** [Planning Worksheet](../week6/CAPSTONE_PLANNING_WORKSHEET.md)

### Step 2: Complete All Sections
Work through the worksheet systematically:

**Phase 1: Initial Planning & Brainstorming**
- Review at least 3 Code.org datasets that interest you
- Brainstorm 1-2 app ideas with target users and value propositions
- Create a 2-minute pitch for your ideas
- Participate in group voting on ideas
- Decide whether to work solo or in a group

**Phase 2: Detailed Planning & Design**
- Finalize your app plan (external data + user data)
- Create wireframes for:
  - Splash screen/home page
  - Data display screen (Report Page)
  - Form page
- Plan your Supabase table structures with columns, data types, and relationships
- Download your chosen Code.org dataset as CSV

**Phase 3: Coding & Implementation Planning**
- Create a 5-day timeline with specific goals for each day
- Complete the technical checklist as you build

### Step 3: Share Your Worksheet
- Make your Google Doc shareable with your instructor
- Ensure all sections are completed
- Add your name and date at the top
